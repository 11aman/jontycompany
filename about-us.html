Welcome To Waste Management Hub

Waste Management Hub set out its journey in the arena of waste management service long time ago, to create a One-Stop Waste Management Solution for the large scale waste generators. We are a Pollution Control Board authorized PRO's. Along with the purchase & collection of Plastic Waste we provides a wide range of value added services which certainly creates a lot of convenience for its clients in managing their scrap/waste. We are currently serving above 10 different manufacturing industries across MADHYA PRADESH. The organization is driven by individuals who are passionate about the cause of recycling and determined to deliver excellent service levels. We are committed towards Compliance & Service Excellence. Our Company is addressing some of the most important needs of its clients who understands the value of a plastic waste recycler and hazardous waste recycler. At our hub we are not just committed to deliver a world class waste management experience, we recognize our responsibility to the world – to better the lives of our business associates, our employees, and the community we serve, and to inspire others to do the same. This commitment to inspiring humanity is how we do business every day.
